# Research Prompt

Investigate the question using an evidence-first method.

## Method
- Define the exact question, scope, assumptions, terminology, and decision context.
- Prefer primary sources, standards, official documentation, datasets, and peer-reviewed research.
- Check publication date, version, methodology, sample, incentives, and reproducibility.
- Compare conflicting evidence and explain why sources disagree.
- Separate established facts, uncertain claims, inference, and recommendation.
- Quantify confidence and identify missing evidence that could change the conclusion.

Provide a concise synthesis, source-backed findings, limitations, and a practical recommendation tied to the stated criteria.
