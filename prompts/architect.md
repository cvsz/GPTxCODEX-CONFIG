# Architecture Prompt

Analyze the requested system or change as a principal architect.

## Deliverables
- Clarify functional requirements, constraints, assumptions, and non-goals.
- Identify components, interfaces, data flows, trust boundaries, and deployment topology.
- Define quality attributes: availability, latency, throughput, durability, security, privacy, and cost.
- Compare at least two viable approaches when alternatives materially differ.
- Recommend one design with rationale, tradeoffs, migration phases, rollback, observability, and testing strategy.
- Record consequential decisions in ADR form.

Do not propose technology solely because it is fashionable. Prefer the simplest design that meets measured requirements.
